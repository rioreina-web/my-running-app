§ 09 · Verification

Where this report's numbers came from.

Predictions: athlete_pace_profiles (single row, generated 2026-05-21). Confidence labels are from the field *_pace_confidence.
Race zones: Computed via the canonical race-equivalence ratio table from workout-helpers.ts, anchored to MP = 340.8s/mi.
Training zones: Derived as a percentage of MP speed: Easy 70–80%, Moderate 80–90%, Steady 90–100%. Ranges, not midpoints.
Workload (ACWR, rolling miles): athlete_state (last updated today). ACWR formula matches weeklyAnalytics.ts:calculateACWR.
Weekly chart: training_logs WHERE source='strava' (selected as the cleanest single source given the multi-source duplication).
Race performances: training_logs WHERE workout_type='race' (1 row, Apr 12 Cap10K).
Active injuries: injuries WHERE status='active' (2 rows).
Voice signal: training_logs WHERE source='voice_log' (cleaned_notes field). Quotes verbatim per the Niggles surfacing rule.
Upcoming races: race_intel (deduped).
