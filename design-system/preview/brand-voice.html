The voice does

  • Sound like a coach who's met you.
  • Use periods. Short sentences.
  • Talk to you. Never "we."
  • Use italic asides — em-dashed.
  • Stay specific: 5 mi, not "five miles."

The voice doesn't

  • Use emoji. Ever.
  • Cheerlead. ("Great job!")
  • Sound like a product brochure.
  • Yell with exclamation points.
  • Capitalize headlines like titles.
