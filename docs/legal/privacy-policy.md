## 3. What We Collect

We collect data in four categories.

### 3.1 Information you provide directly

- **Account data:** email address, name, password hash, authentication tokens (via Sign in with Apple or email/password)
- **Profile data:** experience level, running goals, target races, age group, gender (if you choose to share)
- **Training data:** workout logs, notes, race entries, goals, schedule preferences
- **Voice memos:** audio recordings you create to log or reflect on a workout, plus transcriptions we generate
- **Coaching conversations:** messages you send to the AI coach and its responses
- **Payment info:** handled by Apple App Store, Google Play, or Stripe (if we launch a paid tier) — we do not store your card number

### 3.2 Information synced from third parties you connect

You choose which of these to connect. We only receive data you authorize them to share.

- **Apple HealthKit:** workout data (distance, pace, heart rate, splits, GPS), resting heart rate, sleep data if you enable it
- **Strava:** your activity history and workout metadata
- **Vital / Junction API:** biometric data from connected wearables

### 3.3 Automatically-collected technical data

- **Device data:** iOS version, app version, device model, screen size
- **Usage data:** which screens you view, which features you use, session duration
- **Diagnostic data:** crash reports, performance traces, error logs (via Sentry)
- **IP address:** for rate limiting, abuse prevention, and approximate location

We do not collect precise location unless you explicitly grant permission for GPS tracking during a workout.

### 3.4 Data we derive from other data

The coach works by transforming raw inputs into structured signals. Examples:

- Pace zones computed from your fitness snapshot or race results
- Weekly mileage, ACWR, and load metrics computed from workout logs
- Transcriptions of voice memos into text
- Mood and injury signals extracted from voice memos and notes
- Coaching recommendations generated by AI models using your context

### Important about AI providers

When you send a message to the coach, we send the relevant context (your recent training, paces, goals, recent voice memos) to our AI model providers to generate a response. These providers have their own retention and usage policies:

- We do **not** use athlete-identifying information (email, name) when making AI calls — only internal user IDs and relevant training context.
- We do **not** permit AI providers to use your data to train their public models. This is governed by our enterprise agreements with them.
- Providers may retain inputs and outputs briefly for abuse monitoring (typically 30 days or less), per their policies.

We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not share identifiable data with advertisers or data brokers. Period.

## 5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for these purposes and no others:

- **Run the Service.** Store your training, show you your history, let you log workouts.
- **Give coaching recommendations.** Send relevant context to AI models so the coach can respond usefully.
- **Improve the Service.** Use aggregated, de-identified data to understand what works and what doesn't — which features are used, where users drop off, how to improve the AI coach.
- **Communicate with you.** Transactional emails (confirmations, password resets, major product updates), in-app notifications you've opted into.
- **Keep the Service safe.** Detect abuse, rate-limit API usage, investigate security issues.
- **Comply with legal obligations.** Respond to lawful requests from law enforcement, regulators, or courts.

We do **not** use your data to:

- Build profiles to sell to advertisers
- Train AI models that are then offered to third parties in an identifiable way
- Make automated decisions that have significant legal or similarly significant effects on you without human review

## 8. How We Protect Your Data

- **Encryption in transit:** All communication with our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher.
- **Encryption at rest:** Supabase encrypts data at rest by default.
- **Row-level security (RLS):** Every database query is scoped to the authenticated user. Your data is isolated from other users by default.
- **Minimum-access principle:** Only the subsystems that need your data can access it. AI model calls receive the minimum context required for the task.
- **Secrets management:** API keys and credentials are stored in environment secrets, never in code or logs.
- **Audits:** We periodically review access patterns for anomalies.

We follow industry-standard security practices but cannot guarantee that no breach will ever occur. If we experience a breach affecting your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
